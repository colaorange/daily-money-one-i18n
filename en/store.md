App Name: 
Daily Money One

Short description: 
Daily finance tracking for individuals, families, and small businesses.

Long description:
【Daily Money One】 - Simple, Powerful, Trusted Financial Tracking

Derived from the classic "Daily Money" and "Daily Money+", this is a handcrafted masterpiece by the original author.
We believe accounting shouldn't be a burden, but a way to take control of your life.
This is not an app full of ads or distracting games; it is a clean accounting app designed for "you who take finance seriously".

Why Choose Daily Money One?

✅ Pure & Efficient, No Distractions
No complex social features, no distracting gamification. We provide the most intuitive interface, allowing you to record transactions in seconds.

✅ Practical Accounting Concepts Made Easy
More than just a cash flow log! With a simple structure of Assets, Liabilities, Income, and Expenses, you can easily grasp your "Net Asset" status.
- Hierarchical Accounts: You know your financial structure best. Support unlimited levels of sub-accounts.
- Multiple Books: Keep personal, family, business, or travel expenses completely separate. One App handles all scenarios.
- One-to-Many Split Transactions: Supermarket receipt includes food, household items, and appliances? No problem. One transaction can correspond to multiple expense items, accurately tracking every penny.

✅ Powerful Data Analysis & Budget Control
- Real-time Reports: View Balance Sheets and Charts anytime. Your financial status at a glance.
- Budgets: Set monthly or yearly budgets with real-time progress indicators to effectively control spending and avoid overdrafts.
- Diverse Charts: Trend charts and pie charts for days, weeks, months, and years help you find key savings opportunities.

✅ Exclusive Features
- Server Mode: Phone screen too small? One-click to enable Server Mode. View and analyze all data on your computer browser. Accounting can be professional!
- Convenient Templates & Recurring Schedules: Automate rent, salary, and subscriptions. Set it once, and the system reminds you to record it.
- Excel (XLSX) Export: Need deeper analysis? Export transactions and reports to Excel format. Your data, your rules.

✅ Your Data, Completely Yours
- Open Format: Supports backup to open JSON format. No fear of being locked into a single software.
- Dual Backup: Supports backup to local storage and Google Drive. Your data is safe.
- Privacy Protection: Built-in password lock to secure your financial privacy.

More Thoughtful Details:
- Personalized Themes: Dark Mode and various color themes to switch as you please.
- Data Migration: Full support for importing "Daily Money+" CSV export files. Zero barrier for returning users.
- Completely Free: Developed to solve the author's own needs, it's free for everyone to use.
